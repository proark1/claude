Before you worry

"But I'm not technical…"

Perfect — this was made for you. Every technical word is explained the moment it appears. Try hovering (or tapping) this one: you'll be storing your project in a by the end, and it won't hurt a bit.

🛡️

You can't break anything

Claude asks permission before important steps, and every change can be rewound. Your computer is safe. Promise.

🗣️

Plain English is the tool

"Make the header bigger." "Try warmer colors." That's it. That's the skill — and this tutorial teaches you how to say it well.

🚀

You'll finish with something real

Not a certificate. Not theory. A real website with a real address you can text to your friends tonight.
